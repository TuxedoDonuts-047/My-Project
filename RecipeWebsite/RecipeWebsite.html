This is a guide on how to make a grilled cheese sandwich with bacon and tomato.


Ingredients:

  1. 1. Two pieces of Bacon
  2. 2. One Tomato
  3. 3. Cheddar Cheese
  4. 4. A little bit of Butter
  5. 5. Two slices of Bread (White/Brown/other)


Instructions:

  1. 1. Butter your slices of bread on both sides.
  2. 2. Add cheese on both sides of each slice.
  3. 3. Add bacon and tomato on one slice
  4. 4. Add more cheese.
  5. 5. Put the other slice on top.
  6. 6. Put butter on frying pan. Then wait for pan to heat up.
  7. 7. Put sandwich in frying pan.
  8. 8. Let it set for two minutes.
  9. 9. After that, flip and let it set for another two minutes.
  10. 10.Once the cheese is oozing, taking it out of the frying pan and onto a plate.
  11. 11.Sprinkle some salt and pepper on it.
  12. 12.ENJOY!! ;)
