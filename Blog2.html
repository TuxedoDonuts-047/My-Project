Tuesday March 21st, 2023

On Tuesday 21st of March 2023, I had an interesting day. I walked over to the bus station and went on it to go to Tralee. Once there, I clocked in and left to Manor West Retail Park, Tralee. One of the places I visited was Currys, where I saw the Meta Quest 2 on display. This is a new all-in-one VR headset that offers impressive performance and features. Firstly, it has a Qualcomm Snapdragon XR2 CPU, 6 GB of RAM, and storage options of 128 GB or 256 GB. However, I also knew that the Meta Quest 3 will be released later this year, so I decided to wait for that. After browsing Currys, I went to Costa Coffee and got a Salted Caramel Frappe and a cinnamon bun. I then returned to the College and finished my work for the day.
That was my Tuesday in a nutshell. I hope you enjoyed reading this blog post and stay tuned for more updates from me.
