Friday March 24th, 2023

March 2023, I had an interesting day. I walked over to the bus station and went on it to go to Tralee to go to Munster Technological University (MTU) North Campus.
I had a great time at the MTU North Camps open day on March 24th 2023. I was impressed by the facilities and the staff, especially in the Computing department. I attended a talk that explained what was being done on the course. I also got to try out the VR suite, which was amazing. It was a fun and immersive experience that made me want to learn more about VR technology. I think MTU North Camps is a fantastic place to study Computing and I look forward to applying for a place there.
That was my Friday in a nutshell. I hope you enjoyed reading this blog post and stay tuned for more updates from me.
