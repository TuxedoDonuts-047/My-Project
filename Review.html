Reviews:

Playstation 5:

I've been playing with my PS5 for over a year now and I have to say it's a great console. It's fast, powerful, and has some amazing games that take advantage of the DualSense controller and 3D audio. The load times are almost nonexistent and the UI is sleek and easy to use. The only downsides are the size and the storage. The PS5 is huge and it barely fits in my TV stand. The 825GB SSD also fills up quickly, especially with the new games that are coming out. I got my PS5 at Curry's in February 2021 and I don't regret it, but I wish Sony would make a smaller and cheaper version soon.

Meta Quest 2:

The Meta Quest 2 is a great VR headset that offers a lot of features and immersion. It has a high-resolution display, a powerful processor, and a comfortable design. It also supports a wide range of apps and games, and lets you access the Metaverse with ease. However, it also has some drawbacks, such as the limited battery life, the mandatory Facebook login, and the privacy concerns. Some users also reported glitches and bugs with the software updates. Overall, it is a good device for VR enthusiasts, but it could use some improvements. If you are looking for something better, you might want to wait for the Meta Quest 3, which will be revealed in a live event in September 2023.
